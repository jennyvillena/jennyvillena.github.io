About


I am in my final year at St. Clair College Centre for the Arts working towards an Advanced Diploma in Graphic Design. Over time, I developed a wide variety of designer skills and I discovered an immense talent for vector designs and photography.

Currently, I am working towards taking my passion for Graphic Design to the next level. Utimately, I am excited to provide innovative graphic design solutions to help your business reach its maximum potential.
